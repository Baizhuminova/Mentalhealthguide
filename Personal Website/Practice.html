What to do in case of an alarm ?

Aromatherapy
Some oils, such as lavender, sandalwood or chamomile, have a calming effect. Aromatherapy activates certain receptors, so that the body relaxes, the level of anxiety decreases
The power of breathing
Breathing is the most effective method of quickly reducing anger and anxiety. By adjusting the frequency and depth of breathing, try to slow down the breathing rate for 15 minutes to about 6-7 breaths per minute.
Bring the alarm to an absurd scale
When a person tries to "escape" from an anxious state, his excitement only grows. Psychologists advise to act on the contrary. Imagine that the worst thing happened, what you were most afraid of. Bring the situation to such an absurdity that it causes laughter. Feel how the body relaxes, bad thoughts go away.

What to do when you are stressed ?

Meditation-concentration
Try to concentrate all your attention on a certain object — any thing in sight, or an invented image. During meditation, do not allow any extraneous thoughts, and if this does happen, again shift all attention to the chosen "target". It is much easier for people who regularly practice meditation to keep track of their thoughts and emotions
Write down your thoughts
When anxiety becomes uncontrollable, write down all your thoughts on a blank piece of paper. Look your fears in the face — this will help you assess the seriousness of the situation. This method makes it possible to organize thoughts, calm down and regain self-control
Do cardio exercises
Due to cardio load, the level of serotonin, the hormone of "happiness", increases. To drive away the obsessive anxiety state, do cardio exercises for 7 to 10 minutes. Blood circulation will improve, muscles will tone up, energy will appear

What to do with apathy ?

Meditate
Conscious meditation will help to distract from obsessive thoughts and calm down. There are practices that can be carried out even in crowded places. Sit down, put all your energy there, "disconnect" from the outside world. After a few minutes, you will feel that the anxiety begins to go away
Put on your favorite playlist
Music that gives pleasure can reduce anxiety. A favorite playlist lowers your heart rate, slows down breathing, relieves tension and relaxes. At the same time, the genre does not matter — only the music that you like calms you down
Visualize and practice affirmations
Positive attitudes help to normalize the emotional state. As soon as bad thoughts begin to fill your mind, draw pictures in your imagination that give you joyful emotions. Visualization will help to get rid of anxiety and tune in to the positive. This tactic works especially effectively in combination with affirmations — affirmative positive attitudes
